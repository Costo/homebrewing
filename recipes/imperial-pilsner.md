Imperial Pilsner
================

Batch size: 1 Gal  
Mash time: 60m  
Boil time: 75m  

Grains:  
2.6lbs / 1.180kg of Pilsner malt  
0.15lbs / 68g of Munich malt

Hops: Saaz 1.4oz / 40g  
FWH: 0.18oz / 5g  
60m: 0.21oz / 6g  
30m: 0.28oz / 8g  
15m: 0.35oz / 10g  
0m: 0.39oz / 11g  

Yeast: WLP-802 Lager yeast
